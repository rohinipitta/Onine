About the Book
"Death Note" is a captivating tale written by Tsugumi Ohba and illustrated by Takeshi Obata. This gripping manga series follows the story of Light Yagami, a high school student who discovers a mysterious notebook that grants him the power to kill anyone whose name he writes in it. As Light uses the Death Note to enact his vision of justice, he becomes embroiled in a deadly game of cat and mouse with the enigmatic detective known only as "L." With its intricate plot, moral dilemmas, and intense psychological battles, "Death Note" explores themes of power, morality, and the nature of justice. Originally published in Japanese, this iconic series has been translated into numerous languages, captivating readers around the world with its gripping narrative and thought-provoking themes.
