About the Book
"Music Theory for Guitarists" is an essential guide designed specifically for guitar players aiming to deepen their understanding of music theory. This comprehensive resource covers fundamental concepts such as scales, chords, harmony, and rhythm, providing clear explanations and practical examples tailored to the guitar. With the included online audio resources, readers can enhance their learning experience through interactive exercises and demonstrations. Whether you're a beginner looking to build a strong foundation or an experienced guitarist seeking to expand your musical knowledge, this book offers valuable insights to help you master the fretboard and unlock your full creative potential. Written in English, it serves as a valuable reference for guitarists worldwide.
